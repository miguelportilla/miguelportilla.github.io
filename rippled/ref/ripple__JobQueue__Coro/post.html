JobQueue::Coro::post

Schedule coroutine execution.

Synopsis 
void
post();
Description

Effects: Returns immediately. A new job is scheduled to resume the execution of the coroutine. When the job runs, the coroutine's stack is restored and execution continues at the beginning of coroutine function or the statement after the previous call to yield. Undefined behavior if called consecutively without a corresponding yield.
